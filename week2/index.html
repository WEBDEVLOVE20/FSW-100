Did you know?

Turtles belong to one of the oldest reptile groups in the world, beating snakes, crocodiles and alligators. These creatures date back to the time of the dinosaurs, over 200 million years ago! Some turtles are carnivores, others are herbivores, and some are omnivores. Many baby turtles start life as carnivores but grow to eat more plants as they mature.
